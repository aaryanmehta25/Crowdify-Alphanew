  • Registration

  • How do I change my password?

    Once you go to the sign in page, there will be a redirect link as "forgot my password". Selecting this option will send a link to your registered mail through which you can change your password.

  • How do I sign up?

    On the top right corner of the navigation bar, there will be an option to sign up for the website.

  • Can I unsubscribe to the newsletter

    With every newsletter email, there will be an attached link to unsibscribe to the emails/newsletter.

  • How do donations work?

    Once a user enters an amount to donate, it will be redirected to a donation portal, where all the funds generated will be directly sent to the concerned individual/organisation.

  • Account

  • Can multiple people use the same account?

    No, multiple users will have to sign up using their own respective emails!

  • Can the website open on a phone?

    Yes, there is sharable link available that can open up on any phone, tablet, laptop or pc.

  • Is the newsletter compulsory?

    No, the newsletter is not compulsory. You can sign up for it to be sent on your registered mail daily,weekly or monthly.

  • Payments

  • Can I have an invoice for my donation?

    Yes, the invoice will be generated and sent to your registered email id.

  • Why did my credit card or PayPal payment fail?

    This could be a temporary issue with either the banking portal or in case our website is down due to some software issues.

  • Why does my bank statement show multiple charges for one donation?

    This could be a bank issue. Kindly contact us at our customer care service or contact your bank for further information.

  • Privacy

  • Can I specify my own private password?

    Yes, once you sign up for the website, you log in with your own password everytime, and change it anytime you like.

  • My invoices are missing! How do I get them back?

    All your invoices are available on the "about me" page and you can view them anytime you want.

  • How can I access my account data?

    You can access your account data on the about me page by logging into the website.
